Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

Overview

Smart Heat Tracker ("the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Data We Access

The app requests access to the following Apple HealthKit data types solely to estimate core body temperature:

  • Heart Rate
  • Active Energy Burned
  • Step Count
  • Workout data (activity level)

This data is accessed only when the app is actively in use and only with your explicit permission.

How Data Is Used

All health data is processed entirely on your device. It is used exclusively to compute estimated core body temperature readings and display insights within the app. No data is transmitted to any external server, cloud service, or third party.

Data Storage & Retention

The app does not store health data outside of Apple's HealthKit framework on your device. Deleting the app removes all associated app data. We do not retain any user data externally.

Third-Party Services

Smart Heat Tracker does not integrate any analytics platforms, advertising networks, or third-party tracking services. No SDKs that collect user data are included in the app.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used this app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Your Rights

You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time via iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Smart Heat Tracker. Revoking access will disable core functionality of the app.

Medical Disclaimer

This app is intended for personal informational purposes only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
